If you are interested in Bedford Commercial vehicles, or are the owner of a classic Bedford vehicle, The Bedford Owner and Enthusiast Club aims to provide mutual help in sourcing parts and services, technical information etc. We are based in Cheshire at the Hooton Park Hangars. We maintain our own vehicles and there is a Cherished vehicle Storage facility at Hooton Park.
