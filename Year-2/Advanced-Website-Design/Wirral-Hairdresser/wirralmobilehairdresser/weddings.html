Wedding Hairdressing Services


I specialise in wedding hair and I do offer professional wedding hairdressing packages in Wirral and surrounding area.

Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life, everything must be perfect.

No two weddings are the same and I cater to your individualism whether you wish for a traditional or modern style.

Being a mobile hairdresser in Wirral, it allows me the freedom and flexibility to work closely with you and your bridal party to create the style you want whilst making your special day stress free and as relaxing as possible
