About Us

Jacob the Clown started JB Circus in 2010. His vision to create a show for families that would be remembered for years. Since 2010 the JB Circus has toured the Southeast, moving every week, to entertain and amaze people. JB Circus has become synonymous for the awe inspiring gymnastics that each show is filled with.
